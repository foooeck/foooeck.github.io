about

Since 2022, foec[k] has been promoting feminist values in outdoor sports. Foec[k] encourages Flinta and LGBTQ+ to get out into nature and experience adventure. Fundamental is the idea that humans and "nature" are equal. Being outdoors should reinforce the awareness of this. It is important to make experiences together and to share them.
